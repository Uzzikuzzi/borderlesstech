Approach

Our Global Delivery Model Approach

To meet our client’s present and future challenges we deliver solutions based on a Global Delivery Model approach. This model materializes through Delivery Centers which tailor and provide the right mix of services wherever our customers' needs are. 

Our Delivery Centers

Our Delivery Centers ensure a standardized and industrialized way of delivering your projects.

Delivery Centers are multi-location teams that use common processes, tools and methodologies to deliver local or global projects on a specific expertise and domains. By using Delivery Centers we can maximize the quality of your solutions and projects while also connecting our co-workers to challenging and stimulating work environments.

How do our customers benefit?

  • Access to right & accurate resources

    We provide the right and accurate resources based on the customer project requirements and provide access to highly desired skills that are rare in some markets. We set up dedicated project teams.

  • Scaling possibilities

    We offer different scaling capabilities thanks to our multi-located Delivery Centers and we can quickly scale a team for our customers. We also enable the shift from a local to a global project with global partnership support.

  • Flexible delivery options

    We offer flexible delivery options by offering our customers on-site, near-shore or off-shore delivery options depending on their needs to ensure a mix of cost effectiveness, quality and proximity. We leverage both local and international teams with unified collaboration tools.

  • Highest delivery quality

    We produce the highest quality work possible thanks to the selection of best-skilled engineers scattered around the globe. We offer global collaboration and support with the same tools and processes to unify the collaboration.

  • More work capacity

    We provide more work capacity as teams can hand off work from one location to another at the end of their 'day shift'. Moreover, our offshore teams are used to work on different time zones to ensure high availability of resources.

  • Agility

    We offer fast response to changing customer requirements and project agility.

  • Answers every project lifecycle stage

    We provide all the services portfolio (Advise, Build, Run, Optimize) with flexible engagement modes.

  • Proximity & cost optimization

    We distribute and integrate the workload in a smart manner and deliver services to our customers at an optimal cost. We offer our customers the best balance between proximity, cost, quality and innovation.
